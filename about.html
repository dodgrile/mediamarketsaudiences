Synopsis

Three friends. One podcast obsession. A trail that remembers.

Jade, Stef, and Zoe have been inseparable since college, bound by their shared love of true crime podcasts and weekend hikes. When they discover that the Bones Hollow Trail in Washington State was once the hunting ground of a serial killer who was never caught, they can't resist retracing his victims' final steps.

What starts as a thrilling adventure quickly spirals into paranoia. Strange markers appear on trees. Their GPS fails. Someone—or something—is watching them. As tensions rise and old resentments surface, the friends realize the real danger might not be what's lurking in the woods.

It might be each other.

About the Author

Eliza Jabore is a debut novelist whose love of wilderness horror and psychological suspense culminated in Backstabbers. She holds an MFA in Creative Writing and has worked as a trail guide in the Pacific Northwest, experiences that deeply inform her atmospheric storytelling. She currently lives in the UK, where she continues to explore the darkest corners of human nature—preferably from the safety of her writing desk.

The Campaign

What is the Geocaching Experience?

To celebrate the launch of Backstabbers, we're bringing the story off the page and onto the trails. We've hidden 12 waterproof evidence boxes across the UK's most atmospheric hiking locations—from the Lake District to the Scottish Highlands.

What's Inside Each Box?

  • Exclusive Chapter Excerpt: A non-spoiler scene printed on weathered paper
  • "Evidence" Items: Polaroid photos, trail map fragments, podcast transcripts
  • Character Artifacts: Missing persons flyers, handwritten notes
  • Promotional Items: Bookmarks, QR codes to pre-order, #FindBackstabbers stickers
  • Logbook: Sign your name and share what you'd do if you got lost

Campaign Goals

This geocaching experience aims to:

  • Create an immersive, real-world extension of the book's atmosphere
  • Target the unique overlap between outdoor enthusiasts and thriller readers
  • Generate sustained user-generated content throughout summer 2026
  • Build anticipation for the June 2nd release through discovery and sharing
  • Reward early supporters with exclusive signed bookplates

How to Participate

Visit the interactive map to choose your location, solve the cryptic clues, and discover the hidden boxes. Share your finds on social media with #FindBackstabbers to join the community of seekers.

Important: This campaign follows Leave No Trace principles. All boxes are placed responsibly and will be collected after the campaign period. Please respect the environment and return items to their boxes for other seekers to discover.
